About
✦ Open to Work

I'm Renu — I ask
"why?" a lot.

I love the detective work of research: talking to people, finding patterns in messy data, and uncovering insights that help teams make better decisions.

My approach is simple: understand the problem before solving it. I've done research across travel, fintech, supply chain, and warehouse management — each project teaching me something new about how people think and what they actually need.

I studied interaction design in Barcelona (M.A. from Harbour Space University) and graphic design in Pune (B.Des from Symbiosis Institute of Design). Somewhere along the way, I fell in love with asking questions and finding answers.

When I'm not interviewing users or mapping journeys, I'm probably learning German (B1 in progress), exploring new cities, or convinced I've just asked the perfect research question.
